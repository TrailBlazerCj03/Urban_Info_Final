- To understand the usage and characteristics of the yellow taxi in the borough of Brooklyn in October 2015.
- To understand the relationships between people's behaviors of taking yellow cabs and different zoning features, including Residential Districts(R), Commercial Districts (C), Manufacturing Districts (M), Park Areas (P).
- To visualize the typical trips and identify the populous/typical trips in Brooklyn.
